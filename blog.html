Self-Care Blog

Post Number 1

We often forget to take care of ourselves. It's understandable that that we all have busy lives and rarely have the time to relax and remind ourselves to calm down and take a break.

If you don't take time at least once a week or so, it wont be long till you're stressed and suffering from exhaustion, etc. So there are some ways to take of ourselves, big or small, it can still be very beneficial to us.

Post Number 2

Tiny self-care ideas for the body.

1) Play with a pet, whether its a dog or cat. 2) Take a quick nap or a warm bath 3) Do somethng that energizes your body, whether its stretchingg, swimming, going for jog, or doing whatever type of physical activity that you enjoy.

Post Number 3

Not only is it important to take care of yourself physically, but as well as mentally .

Some ideas that can benefit your mind can be 1) Punctuate your day with a "mini-medidation" - a minute of being aware of your thoughts, feelings and sensation. 2) Wander around - exploring the city or anywhere you've never been to helps and look for its beauty you've never noticed before
